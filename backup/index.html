About Me

Turning Ideas into
Bold Visuals

I'm Kawesi Tauba, a graphic designer based in Kampala, Uganda trading as Taubest. I'm passionate about creating designs that don't just look good — they work hard for your brand.

Whether you're a startup needing your first logo or an established business refreshing your look, I bring creativity, precision, and local understanding to every project. No templates. No generic work. Every design is built from scratch for you.

My philosophy is simple: great design should be accessible. You don't need a big budget to have a professional brand.
