Common Questions

Everything you need
to know.

Still have questions? Just WhatsApp me directly — I reply within the hour.

How long does a website take?
Most websites are ready within 48 hours of me receiving your details — business name, services, photos and location. More complex sites take 3–5 days. I always communicate clearly on timelines before starting.
How do I pay?
50% upfront via Mobile Money (MTN or Airtel) before I start, and 50% on delivery when you approve the final site. No hidden fees — what we agree is what you pay.
What if I want changes after it's live?
One round of revisions is included in every package. Need to update a price or add a service later? My monthly maintenance plan covers ongoing updates for UGX 80,000/month.
Will my site show on Google?
Every site I build includes basic SEO setup — meta tags, Google Search Console submission and indexing request. Premium and add-on packages include Google My Business setup too.
Do I need technical knowledge?
None at all. You give me your details on WhatsApp — I handle everything. Building, deploying, setting up your domain and making it live. You just review and approve.
Can you work with businesses outside Kampala?
Yes — I work remotely across Uganda. All communication and file sharing happens on WhatsApp and Google Drive, so location doesn't matter. I've worked with businesses all over Uganda.
